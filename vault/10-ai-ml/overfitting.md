## Summary

When a model learns the noise in the training data rather than the underlying pattern, resulting in poor generalization to unseen data.

## Key points

* High training performance but poor validation/test performance
* Caused by excessive model complexity or insufficient training data
* Prevented by techniques like regularization, dropout, and early stopping
